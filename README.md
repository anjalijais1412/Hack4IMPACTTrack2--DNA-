# Hack4IMPACTTrack2--DNA-
Team Name:- DNA<br>
Member Name:- Anjali Jaiswal, Nisha Singh and Dhruv Bafana<br>
Domain:-Green Infrastructure & Smart Cities<br>
Approved Problem Statement:-Urban areas face inefficient solid waste management, with a large portion of organic waste remaining underutilized, causing environmental pollution and landfill overflow. Citizens often miss waste collection due to lack of timely alerts, resulting in improper disposal and reduced system efficiency. This problem affects municipalities, citizens, and the environment, making it critical to develop a sustainable, smart and automated solution.
